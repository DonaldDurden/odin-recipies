Swedish Pancakes

Mixture

  • 4 Large eggs
  • 2 Cups milk
  • 1/2 Cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar(Optional)
  • 1 Pinch of salt
  • Butter for the skillet

Step 1

Mix salt and flour(and sugar)

Step 2

Combine with milk

Step 3

Add eggs

Step 4

Cook in a medium heat skillet, add batter to then spread it even with the skillet.

Step 5

Enjoy
